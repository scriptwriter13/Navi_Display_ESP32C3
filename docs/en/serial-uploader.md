# Serial Uploader Guide

Here you can flash the firmware directly to the ESP32-C3 via your browser.

## Requirements
- A browser with Web Serial support (Chrome, Edge, Opera).
- A USB-C cable capable of data transfer.

## Steps
1. Connect the ESP32-C3 to your PC.
2. Click "Connect".
3. Select the port.
4. Click "Install".
